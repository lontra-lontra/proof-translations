\begin{abstract}
 65
This work aims to discuss the translation between Tableaux proofs and sequent calculus proofs in first-order intuitionistic predicate logic and in first-order classical predicate logic.
 66
It begins with an overview of the definitions and clarification of the notation.
 67
It then shows a translation process in classical logic and its OCAML implementation restricted to propositional logic.
 68
Finally, a potential extension towards translation in intuitionistic logic is discussed.
